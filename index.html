Welcome to our cottage!

  • Beautiful 4 bedroom cottage 75 feet from pristine lakefront
  • Perfect multigenerational escape with easy accessibility first floor bedroom and shower, plus 3 additional bedrooms and bathroom upstairs
  • Oversized Shore Master dock perfect for sunning, swimming, fishing or mooring your boat.
  • Easy access boat launch just a short ride away

We discovered the Belgrade Lakes area a few years ago, on a beautiful autumn day. After exploring the small town of Belgrade, we hiked French Mountain* and made plans to return the following summer for a longer family vacation. That vacation sealed the deal. After renting our fair share of cottages over the years, we knew we wanted a place of our own that we could also share with others.

Our space is ideal for larger family vacations, reunions or Colby College events. We are situated near Colby College and Belgrade Lakes Golf Course and just a short drive to Sugarloaf, Bar Harbor, Acadia National Park, and Freeport (LL Bean and outlets).
